Air Fryer Hot Dogs

Description

You know them, you love them, you can't quit them. Even when you try.

Hot dogs, baby.

Ingredients:

  • 4 hot dog buns. Or, a number of hot dog buns
  • 4 hot dogs. Or, a number of hot dogs. Preferably the same amount of buns. See picture above... DAVE!

Steps:

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. It places the buns in a single layer in the air fryer basket; cooks them in the preheated air fryer until crisp, about 2 minutes, and removes the buns to a plate or fancy chopping board before...
  3. ...That's right, placing the hot dogs in a single layer in the air fryer basket; cooking them for 3 minutes, then removing them to serve the hot dogs in the toasted buns before it gets the hose again.
